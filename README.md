# Local SEO Audit and Fix Plan
### Screaming Frog + Google Search Console | Home Services Business

---

## Overview

This project documents a technical and on-page SEO audit conducted for a local home services business operating across multiple service areas. The business offers residential plumbing, HVAC, and electrical services across a metro region with several suburb-level service pages.

The audit was designed to identify crawlability issues, indexation gaps, on-page optimisation weaknesses, and missed local keyword opportunities. Findings were translated into a prioritised fix backlog, a service page optimisation checklist, and an ongoing monitoring plan.

> **Note:** All business names, URLs, and metrics in this portfolio are demo assumptions used to illustrate real audit methodology. They do not represent a real client.

---

## Audit Goal

- Identify technical issues preventing search engine bots from properly crawling and indexing the site
- Diagnose on-page weaknesses on service and location pages that reduce local ranking ability
- Surface quick-win CTR improvements using Google Search Console data
- Deliver a prioritised, actionable fix roadmap that a developer and content writer can execute without ambiguity

## Summary of Findings

The audit surfaced **47 actionable issues** across four categories. Below is a high-level breakdown.

### Technical Issues (14 issues)
- 9 pages returning 3xx redirects that should be updated to point directly to canonical URLs
- 3 orphaned service pages with no internal links pointing to them
- 2 pages blocked in robots.txt that should be indexable (service area pages)

### On-Page Issues (18 issues)
- 11 service pages with duplicate or templated title tags (e.g. "Plumbing Services | CompanyName" repeated across suburb variants)
- 7 pages missing H1 tags or using H2 as the primary heading
- 6 pages with thin content under 300 words
- 4 pages missing a primary call-to-action above the fold

### Local SEO Issues (9 issues)
- No LocalBusiness or Service schema markup across any page
- NAP (Name, Address, Phone) inconsistent across 3 external directories vs the website footer
- Location modifier absent from title tags on 8 suburb service pages
- Google Business Profile category misaligned with highest-volume service

### Internal Linking Issues (6 issues)
- Homepage links to only 4 of 14 service pages
- No breadcrumb navigation implemented
- Service category hub pages lack links to suburb variants

## Summary of Action Plan

Fixes were sequenced using a Priority Score combining SEO impact, business impact, and implementation effort.

### Phase 1 - Quick Wins (Weeks 1-2)
- Fix robots.txt to unblock service area pages
- Resolve redirect chains to direct canonical URLs
- Add location modifiers to title tags on suburb pages

### Phase 2 - Core On-Page (Weeks 3-5)
- Rewrite title tags and H1s for all service pages using local keyword templates
- Add unique introductory copy to templated location pages
- Implement LocalBusiness and Service schema on all key pages

### Phase 3 - Content and Structure (Weeks 6-10)
- Build or expand thin service pages to 600+ words with local intent signals
- Create internal linking map and update hub and spoke structure
- Add breadcrumb navigation sitewide

### Phase 4 - Monitoring and Iteration (Ongoing)
- Weekly GSC coverage checks
- Monthly crawl health verification
- Track KPI movement on target service + location keyword combinations
