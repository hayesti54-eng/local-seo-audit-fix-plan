# Service Page Optimisation Checklist
### Local Home Services SEO | Per-Page Quality Standard

---

## How to Use This Checklist

Run this checklist for every service page and location-specific service page before it is published or after it has been updated. A page should pass all items in the Core section before moving to Phase 2 of the fix roadmap.

Mark each item as:
- `[x]` Passes - no action needed
- `[ ]` Fails - fix required before sign-off
- `[N/A]` Not applicable to this page type

## 1. Title Tag

- [ ] Title tag is present and not empty
- [ ] Title tag is under 60 characters (check in SERPs preview tool)
- [ ] Title tag contains the primary service keyword (e.g. "emergency plumber")
- [ ] Title tag contains the location modifier (suburb, city, or area name)
- [ ] Title tag contains the brand name, placed at the end
- [ ] Title tag follows the recommended pattern: `[Primary Keyword] in [Location] | [Brand Name]`
- [ ] Title tag is unique - does not duplicate any other page on the site
- [ ] Title tag does not use keyword stuffing or pipe-separated keyword lists

**Recommended Pattern Examples:**
- `Emergency Plumber in Springfield | Apex Plumbing`
- `Drain Cleaning Services in Riverside | Apex Plumbing`
- `Hot Water System Repair Near Me | Same-Day | Apex Plumbing`

## 2. H1 Heading

- [ ] H1 tag is present on the page
- [ ] Only one H1 exists on the page (check source or Screaming Frog H1 report)
- [ ] H1 contains the primary service keyword
- [ ] H1 contains the location modifier or a direct reference to the service area
- [ ] H1 appears above the fold (visible without scrolling on desktop and mobile)
- [ ] H1 is distinct from the title tag (not an exact copy - variation is preferred)
- [ ] H1 reads naturally and is not keyword-stuffed

**Recommended Pattern Examples:**
- `Expert Emergency Plumbing in Springfield`
- `Professional Drain Cleaning Services - Riverside and Surrounding Areas`
- `Same-Day Hot Water Repairs for Springfield Homeowners`

**Notes:**

---

## 3. Local Modifiers

- [ ] The suburb or city name appears in the title tag
- [ ] The suburb or city name appears in the H1
- [ ] The suburb or city name appears within the first 100 words of body copy
- [ ] The service area is referenced at least twice in the body content
- [ ] Nearby suburbs or neighbourhoods are mentioned where relevant (supports geographic relevance)
- [ ] Service area description does not read as keyword spam - context is natural
- [ ] If the business serves multiple areas, a "Areas We Serve" section or list is present

**Notes:**

---

## 4. Unique Copy

- [ ] Page has a minimum of 500 words of unique body content
- [ ] Content is not copy-pasted from another service page or suburb variant
- [ ] Opening paragraph explains who the page is for, what service is offered, and where
- [ ] Content references local context (suburb characteristics, local licensing, common local issues)
- [ ] At least one suburb-specific sentence is present that could not apply to any other location page
- [ ] No lorem ipsum or placeholder text is visible
- [ ] Readability is appropriate for a homeowner audience (aim for Grade 8-10 reading level)

**Content Depth Check:**
- [ ] Word count is 500 words or above
- [ ] Subheadings (H2, H3) are used to break up content and aid scanning
- [ ] At least one section addresses common customer questions or concerns about the service

## 5. Service Trust Signals

Trust signals reduce friction and increase the likelihood that a visitor calls or submits a form.

- [ ] At least one customer review or testimonial is visible on the page
- [ ] The number of years in business or founding year is mentioned
- [ ] Relevant trade licences or certifications are listed (e.g. licensed plumber / gas fitter)
- [ ] Any guarantees or warranties are clearly stated (e.g. "100% satisfaction guarantee")
- [ ] Recognised accreditations or memberships are mentioned (e.g. Master Plumbers Association)
- [ ] Insurance information is referenced (important for home services customers)
- [ ] A Google Business Profile rating or review count is visible (via embedded widget or reference)
- [ ] Photos of completed jobs or the service team are present and relevant to the service

## 6. Call-to-Action Clarity

- [ ] At least one primary CTA is visible above the fold on desktop
- [ ] At least one primary CTA is visible above the fold on mobile
- [ ] The CTA uses specific action language (e.g. "Call Now for a Free Quote" not just "Contact Us")
- [ ] The phone number is displayed prominently and is a clickable `tel:` link on mobile
- [ ] A secondary CTA (online form or booking option) is available below the fold
- [ ] The CTA colour contrasts clearly with the background - does not blend in
- [ ] If there is an emergency service offering, the CTA reflects urgency (e.g. "24/7 Emergency Line")

**CTA Language Examples for Home Services:**
- "Call [Phone] for Same-Day Service in Springfield"
- "Get a Free Quote Today - No Call-Out Fee"
- "Book Online or Call 24/7"
- "Speak to a Licensed Plumber Now"

## 7. Internal Links

- [ ] The page includes at least 3 internal links to other relevant pages on the site
- [ ] At least one link points to a related service page (e.g. from drain cleaning to pipe repair)
- [ ] At least one link points upward to the service category hub page
- [ ] At least one link points to the contact or quote request page
- [ ] If this is a suburb service page, it links to other services available in the same suburb
- [ ] If this is a service page (not suburb-specific), it links to the suburb variants of that service
- [ ] All internal link anchors are descriptive - no "click here" or "read more" anchors
- [ ] Breadcrumb navigation is present and correct (e.g. Home > Plumbing > Emergency Plumbing > Springfield)

**Notes:**

## 8. FAQ and Schema

### FAQ Content

- [ ] Page includes a FAQ section with at least 3 questions (recommended for local service pages)
- [ ] FAQ questions match real queries found in GSC or keyword research (not generic)
- [ ] Each FAQ answer is concise (2-4 sentences) and directly answers the question
- [ ] FAQs are relevant to the specific service and location where possible

**FAQ Question Examples for Home Services:**
- "How quickly can you respond to an emergency plumbing call in Springfield?"
- "Do you offer a free quote for drain cleaning services?"
- "Are your plumbers licensed and insured in [State]?"
- "What areas do you cover for same-day hot water repairs?"

### Schema Markup

- [ ] LocalBusiness JSON-LD schema is present on the page (or inherited from homepage/template)
- [ ] Service schema is implemented with at minimum: name / description / areaServed / provider
- [ ] If FAQ section is present, FAQPage schema is implemented matching the visible questions and answers
- [ ] BreadcrumbList schema is present and reflects the actual page hierarchy
- [ ] AggregateRating schema is present if reviews are displayed
- [ ] Schema passes validation in Google's Rich Results Test with no errors
